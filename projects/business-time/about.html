About Lipstick Revolt


Lipstick Revolt is a women empowerment organization. We highlight and promote talented females by creating a platform where their voices are heard through media, events, panels, workshops, and more.

Starting out as a response to the lack of knowledge on women, and the question of whether talnted women even existed, Lipstick Revolt came to existence. We have promoted hundreds of women and look forward to helping millions more.

We seek understanding between men and women, and aim to create an environment filled with love, balance, and unity. There is no male bashing and we encourage men to support and be a part of the empowerment of women.
